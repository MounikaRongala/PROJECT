ABSTRACT

The aim of this survey is to introduce an interdisciplinary project about air pollution that is organized and applied for community service. In order to organize this project, we conduct a survey with people in industrial areas and bring awareness toward air pollution. The results indicate the need to make people aware of fundamental concepts, causes-effects, and precautions in order to reduce air pollution.

The global environment faces a major issue of air pollution, posing serious threats to living beings, ecosystems, and climate—especially in densely populated urban areas.

Health Impacts of Air Pollution:

* Respiratory Issues
* Cardiovascular Problems
* Cancer
* Neurological Effects
* Allergic Reactions

Questions for Analysis:

• What do you think air pollution is?
• What could be the reasons behind air pollution?
• What are the potential results of air pollution?
• What steps can be taken to prevent air pollution?
• What are you willing to do to reduce air pollution?
